Important abbreviations
========================
* DOY: Day of the Year
* EOF: Empirical Orthogonal Function
* MJO: Madden-Julian Oscillation
* OLR: Outgoing long-wave radiation
* OMI: OLR-based MJO index.
* PC: Principal Component
